The Biography

M3 MUSIC ARTIST emerged from the digital underground during the 2021 M3 Hackathon. By blending high-frequency electronic textures with relentless rhythmic loops, M3 has created a "perpetual sound" philosophy.

The mission is simple: eliminate the silence. Through the 2021 Hackathon portal, M3 has reached listeners globally, providing a 24/7 auditory experience that never resets.
